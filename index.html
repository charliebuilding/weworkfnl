VIK Partnership Structure

The Exchange.

No money changes hands. WeWork provides access to its London network and promotional channels. FNL provides brand exposure at Run The Wharf and across its community. A clean value-for-value swap.

WeWork Provides to FNL
FNL promotional materials (flyers, posters, QR codes) displayed across agreed WeWork London locations
Distribution of FNL event information via WeWork member communications (email newsletters, app notifications)
Access to WeWork community event slots — FNL to host "Lunch & Learn" or networking sessions promoting events
Digital screen promotion in WeWork common areas across key London locations
FNL discount codes shared with WeWork members — driving race sign-ups directly from the network
Co-branded social media posts from WeWork London channels promoting FNL events
Access to WeWork event spaces for FNL partnership meetings, sponsor activations, or community gatherings
FNL Provides to WeWork
Branded activation space at Run The Wharf for WeWork to promote memberships and workspace tours
Co-branded social media content across FNL's 120K+ following
WeWork member discount on FNL race entries — exclusive "WeWork × FNL" entries
Value in Kind
Zero Cash. Maximum Reach.
FNL accesses WeWork's 40+ London locations and member network. WeWork accesses FNL's 50,000+ runners and 120K+ social following at Run The Wharf. Both brands grow — together.
